What It Is

A peer group that actually understands the CVC world.

The CVC Mastermind is designed for Corporate Venture Capital investors. The CVC world has its own distinct dynamics: navigating internal politics, mandate constraints, LP-equivalent relationships with the parent corporation, and the challenge of maintaining a venture mindset inside a large organisation.

A peer group that truly understands that context doesn't yet exist. This cohort is the first.

Not a generic VC programme
Not an accelerator
Not another LinkedIn group
Who It's For

For CVC Principals, Directors, and VPs

Active portfolio managers navigating the intersection of corporate and venture.

Managing an Active Portfolio

CVC principals, directors, and VPs with hands-on portfolio responsibilities and real investment decisions to make.

Navigating Mandate & Alignment

Investors defining mandates or managing internal stakeholder expectations within their parent corporation.

Seeking Peer Thinking

CVCs looking for genuine peer perspective and candid conversation outside their corporate bubble.

What You Get

Everything You Need to Navigate the CVC Role

01

Bi-Weekly Group Sessions

Structured, facilitated 90-minute conversations designed to break through your most pressing CVC challenges.


  • Mandate strategy + narrative

Hotseat Coaching

One member brings their most pressing challenge. The group engages without hierarchy or judgment — a confidential board without politics. Dedicated time to get real thinking on what's actually keeping you up at night.

03

Expert Sessions

Guest facilitators from LP, corporate innovation, and ecosystem networks. Topics chosen around what the cohort actually needs — not a generic VC curriculum. Select guests from the EU.vc network.

04

1:1 On-Call Coaching

On-demand support between sessions for high-stakes moments and decisions. Because timing matters when you're navigating something that can't wait until the next group call.

05

London In-Person Day

One full-day gathering in London in September. Deeper work, relationship building, and a guest session. The day where the cohort becomes a real peer network — exclusive to this cohort.

Selection Process

Why Not Everyone Gets In

This only works if people can speak freely. That requires strict confidentiality and zero competitive overlap.

  • Confidentiality is non-negotiable
  • No direct competitive overlap — by sector or parent company
  • A shared commitment to candor, transparency and contribution
  • Willingness to be challenged
  • Maximum 8 members per cohort — curation is the product
