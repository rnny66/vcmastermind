What It Is

A trusted board of peers + a skilled facilitator holding the space.

A small, hand-picked circle of emerging managers who meet bi-weekly to get sharper, calmer, and more effective as GPs — without the noise, posturing, or competitiveness of public VC communities.

This is not a transactional networking group. The value lies in clear thinking, better decisions, and not feeling like you're building your fund alone. A reference group where shared realities replace pitching and decision-making quality compounds.

Selection Process

Why Not Everyone Gets In

This only works if people can speak freely, and that requires confidentiality and zero competitive overlap.

  • Confidentiality is non-negotiable
  • No direct fund competition (geo, strategy)
  • A shared commitment to candor, transparency and contribution
  • Willingness to be challenged
  • We do not include people looking to pitch, recruit or build a brand
