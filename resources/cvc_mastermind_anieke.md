The idea

The Corporate Venture Capital Manager Mastermind, is designed for Corporate Venture Capital investors. The CVC world has its own distinct dynamics: navigating internal politics, mandate constraints, LP-equivalent relationships with the parent corporation, and the challenge of maintaining a venture mindset inside a large organisation. A peer group that truly understands that context doesn't yet exist. This cohort would be the first.

Who it's for

* CVC principals, directors, and VPs managing an active portfolio  
* Investors navigating mandate definition or internal stakeholder alignment  
* CVCs looking for peer thinking outside their corporate bubble  
* No direct competitive overlap between members (by sector or parent company)
