Our Approach

Leadership in venture capital is not only strategic — it is personal, relational, and deeply human.

We facilitate the conversations that matter, and we create space for reflection, honesty, and the occasional moment of levity. In our experience clarity doesn't always emerge through analysis. Sometimes it shows up through connection, creativity, and the freedom to be fully yourself in the room.

The Work

What We Explore Together

The work, across three months.

  • Power dynamics inside the fund: how decisions actually get made, where you stand, and how to show up with authority before the title confirms it
  • Your zone of genius: what you're uniquely good at, and how to build from that rather than compensating for what you're not
  • Your investment POV: what you actually believe about capital, risk, and the future, and how to articulate it with confidence
  • Difficult conversations, done well: using brain-based principles to navigate high-stakes moments with partners, LPs, and founders
  • Stakeholder dynamics: understanding what GPs, LPs, and portfolio founders actually need from you at this stage
