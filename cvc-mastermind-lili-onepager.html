The Problem

CVC investors operate at the intersection of two worlds — and are fully understood by neither.

  • ▸ Mandate ambiguity — strategic value vs. financial return? The answer shifts with every leadership change.
  • ▸ Managing upward — parent acts as LP but can veto deals, redirect strategy, or shut the unit down.
  • ▸ Internal politics — a portfolio company vs. a business unit is a political problem, not just an investment one.
  • ▸ Ecosystem isolation — misunderstood by independent VCs and corporate peers alike.
  • ▸ Career uncertainty — what if the unit is restructured overnight?

A peer group that genuinely understands this context — with no corporate agendas, no competitive overlap, no performance — does not yet exist. We've designed this programme to be that.
